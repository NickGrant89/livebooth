# 01 — Product Vision

## One-liner

**LiveBooth** is a live streaming platform built exclusively for DJs — stream sets, earn $DROP on VeChain, unlock achievements, and monetize through tips, subs, and DJ-native features Kick and Twitch don't offer.

## Problem

| Stakeholder | Pain |
|-------------|------|
| **DJs** | General platforms (Kick, Twitch) aren't built for music. DMCA takedowns, no track metadata, no request queues, poor discovery by genre/BPM. Payouts are slow and geographically limited. |
| **Fans** | Hard to support DJs directly during a set. No way to unlock track IDs, request songs, or prove superfan status. |
| **Platform** | Music streaming is underserved; a DJ-focused vertical with crypto-native payments creates a defensible niche. |

## Solution

A Kick-style live platform where:

- DJs broadcast audio/video sets with DJ-specific tooling
- Fans tip and interact with DROP tokens in real time
- Achievements reward growth milestones for DJs and fans
- Unique features (track IDs, crowd requests, collab mode) create moat

## Target Users

### Primary — DJs
- Bedroom DJs building an audience
- Club/resident DJs streaming weekly sets
- Genre specialists (techno, DnB, house, hip-hop, etc.)
- B2B/collab pairs

### Secondary — Fans / Listeners
- Ravers and club-goers who can't attend in person
- Music discovery enthusiasts
- Crypto-native users who prefer token tips over PayPal

### Tertiary — Partners
- Record labels (cleared streams)
- Hardware brands (Pioneer, Denon, Native Instruments)
- Clubs and event promoters (residency slots)

## Positioning

```
                    General streaming
                           │
         Kick ─────────────┼──────────── Twitch
                           │
                           │  LiveBooth
                           │  (DJ-native + crypto)
                           ▼
                    Music / DJ vertical
```

| Platform | Focus | LiveBooth advantage |
|----------|-------|----------------------|
| Kick | General live | DJ workflows, track economy, genre discovery |
| Twitch | Gaming + IRL | Better creator split, music-first UX |
| Mixcloud | Uploads/VOD | Live interaction, crypto tips, achievements |
| SoundCloud | Tracks | Live sets, real-time community |

## Success Metrics (Year 1 targets)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Active DJs streaming/month | 500 |
| Concurrent live streams (peak) | 50 |
| Monthly DROP tip volume | 100,000 DROP |
| Avg stream duration | 90 min |
| DJ 30-day retention | 40% |
| Fan wallet connect rate | 25% of active viewers |

## Brand

- **Name:** LiveBooth
- **Token:** DROP (DropToken)
- **Accent:** `#53fc18` (Kick-inspired green, music/rave energy)
- **Tone:** Underground but accessible. Not corporate. Built by people who go to clubs.

## Non-goals (v1)

- Competing with Spotify for on-demand music
- Full DEX / speculative token trading at launch
- Mobile native apps (web-first)
- Gambling/casino categories
